ABOUT US

4th Marine Raiders Reenactment is a team based solely off MARSOC kits. 4th was inspired after watching a reenactment community of MARSOC crumble. When you look at a community of reenactment what do we see the most? Navy Seals, Devgru, Rangers and even CAG has been getting bigger.

First we looked into real Raider battalions, and noticed there was only a 1st, 2nd and a 3rd Bat. We decided on 4th that way we did not cross any water that may be unwanted of going off a real MARSOC battalion. The team has grown since its beginning which was a rough beginning as there was some lash back. After the slander, we continued to build we are currently spreading internationally from Chile, to Italy and more! We also have partnered teams as well.

Since our beginning we have began donating to the Raider Foundation, and other charity programs that help our men and women that have made the sacrifice, that have made the choice to serve so that we could enjoy this great country!

RECRUITMENT

4th Marine Raider Reenactment Battalion is international and is always looking for members to join our teams and make teams. We are all over the world and hope to continue to spread!

Current Groups:

  • MRT 4011 - AZ
  • MRT 4024 - Georgia
  • MRT 4031 - Netherlands
  • MRT 4040 - Italy
  • MRT 4042 - Italy
  • MRT 4050 - Chile
  • MRT 4055 - Poland
  • MRT 4060 - Latvija
  • MRT 4094 - Serbia

If you are interested in joining any of the these teams please be ready to meet gear requirements.

Requirements are:

  • MARSOC kits (not year specific, preferred but not required)
  • 4 + members
  • Facebook Page 4th MRRB - 40__ (2 numbers of your choice and a name if you like so e.i 4th MRRB - 4051 Striker One)

  • NO stolen valor insignia, meaning no wearing USMC tabs, ranks, etc. MARSOC patch is allowed but still a very thin line!

4th MRRB is a team that is dedicated to MARSOC reenactment, while at the same time still upholding respect for the real Marine Raiders. When team patches are ordered profit of that goes to the Raider Foundation. If you are wearing a MARSOC patch, please try to buy a 4th patch as soon as, if your team does not have teams patches already.

During anytime admins of 4th MRRB find material on the page that may be considered stolen valor, you will be asked to remove that from your page. Failure to do so will result of a revoke of your pages and partnership with 4th MRRB.

We thank you for you interest and hope to hear from you very soon!
